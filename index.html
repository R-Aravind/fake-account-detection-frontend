ABOUT FRIEND OR FAKE

Recently, the popularity of online social networks (OSNs) has increased at a surprising rate, with several millions of users daily. They have turned out to be the fundamental parts of our online lives, employing them as a medium to connect with people around the world, share news, express views, and advertise products. But with its rapid growth, fake profiles and accounts started materializing abruptly, and the cybercriminals resorting to carrying out defaming propaganda on a large scale against individuals and organizations catalyzed new security threats to the OSN users because of the massive amount of personal information they share. Hence, to combat these actions and maintain the security and privacy of OSN users, this project focuses on the detection of fake social media presence on the internet.
